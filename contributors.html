Zakk Haug
Zakk's Github Zakk's Instagram

Zakk is a young coding student enrolled in the UPENN bootcamp. He is previously a music teacher out of Woods Services, as well as a carpenter/electrician apprentice. He currently lives in Morrisville, PA & spends most of his time working & studying. In his free time he enjoys spending time with his fiance Sam, his dog Nala, and his two cats Zombie & Po. Zakk is also an avid LEGO builder, & frequently showcases his work on Instagram & at a local shop. Zakk is also very into gaming; his favorite games include Halo, Sonic, Destiny, Super Smash Bros, Rocket League, CoD, and Pokemon. He can usually be found online playing Smash Bros. or Destiny with his friends.

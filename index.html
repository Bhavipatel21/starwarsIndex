An Index of the Galaxy


Brought to you by Bhavika, Kajal & Zakk


It is a time of civil unrest, and renegade paragraphs floating through space. The Star Wars fanbase is in squabbles over the reception of the "Sequel Trilogy." Some claim George Lucas should never have sold the rights to Disney, while others claim Disney is creating a far more expansive universe than Lucas could have, all while new & younger fans continue to join.

Amidst this turmoil and chaos, three students of the UPENN Coding Bootcamp have been dispatched to create an encyclopedia of knowledge, encompassing as much obtainable information in a simple, familiar format, in the hopes of restoring some sense of peace to the fanbase...
